How can we ensure successful migration?

Here are the steps to ensure successful migration according AWS:

Planning thoroughly for your migration and have a really good strategy

A well-though-out strategy thinks about how ready the company is according to the technology and environment, while making full use of Migration Acceleration Program also known as ‘MAP’ to help create the layout of the mass migration

Choosing the right person to team with

Finding the right person can help make it easier for your company’s migration to cloud, this person has to have a good track, and is align with the company’s goal record, open to discuss anything regarding the migration to help save time in the long run.

Being efficient with what you are paying for, so there is no hidden cost

Because you are migrating to cloud, it will cost a lot, so you need manage the cost properly, making sure ongoing progress is within predicted cost so that you are not surprise when seeing the bill at the end of the month, and also monitoring using amazon cloud watch and tagging.

Using a software that help you, and what part should you migrate

Using tools like Migration Evaluator gives awareness to your current platform, give time to picking tools or multiple of them for your environment, making sure the tools uses gives insights of the goal of the company and that is really reliable

Prioritizing application that need to be used:

Pilling up the application by the goal of the company’s, such as types of application that is going to be used, making comparison between internal and external application, which is ideal for what before migration as this cuts down the time to debugging an application.

Creating a copy of your environment, and testing it before proceeding

Creating a copy of your environment testing it before the actual migration, this will assist you for the consistency and reliability to make an ideal outcome

Making sure your team is trained and taught properly to be well prepared:

Make sure your technical staff that is handling the migration is well trained.

Getting ready for organization environment exchange

Preparing the staff members for the environment, for different roles in the cloud service, without giving full access to the admin access.
