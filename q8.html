What are the concerns and issues encounter by others?

According to the companies below, they have encountered some concerns and issues when migrating to AWS.

1. Aspire Systems

When migrating to AWS cloud, the following were the major challenges they need to overcome:

  • Multiplicity of platforms and applications which needs to be migrated to AWS
  • Technical challenges (deployment architecture)
  • Complex solution architecture and redundant data of tightly-coupled applications
  • Difficulties in migrating to a newer framework incrementally
  • Security
  • Developer and management tools
  • Performance issues related to on-premise data centres

2. Netflix

When Netflix has decided to go all-in to AWS cloud, they faced a lot of outages as cloud computing is not perfect either. Failures are ultimately not possible to avoid in any large-scale distribution system. Despite that, cloud computing has made it possible to survive from failures without affecting the member experience. The benefits when moving to AWS cloud is crystal clear, but it still took Netflix seven years to complete their migration as there are a lot of work and factors that need to be considered when moving to cloud. Netflix has also decided to rebuilt all of their technology and change the operation of the whole company radically as their cloud-native approach as they figured moving their existing systems to AWS has a lot of limitations and problems.
