Our organization has decided to migrate. How do we choose the right provider? Criteria:

Business Requirements

Choose a cloud service provider suitable for our business requirement. Vendors will offer us with a group of specifications; choose the plans which will suit our business needs. during this way, we will quickly create a shortlist of suppliers. it's easy to eliminate those providers who don't match to our business needs.

Cost:

Since the method of cloud migration is expensive, choosing the proper plan may be a crucial decision. Specify the precise amount of cash the corporate is ready to spend on cloud infrastructure then compare the few selected cloud service provider pricing plans and licenses.

Data Security

Categorise what data goes into the cloud. Ensure data protection if you will keep a couple of sensitive data. Also, verify the physical location of the server, if you propose to put an enormous volume of knowledge within the cloud. Before choosing a cloud solution provider, check their data security compliance and ensure whether or not they can avoid data breaches.

Technology Advancements

Choose a service provider who is advanced in technical competencies and operational capabilities. Verify whether the cloud service provider offers the technology that aligns with our needs. Also, make sure the presence of skilled personnel for the requested technology. a couple of cloud providers subcontract the technology options, which can be advantages during a few situations and a drawback in many others.

Support

Cloud service provider who offers 24 by 7 support. knowledgeable help during the migration and deployment may be a must. we will find third-party partners that have adequate experience of the target platform and can address unforeseen issues, but they can’t provide the support they have. In any case, before we sign a deal, we must know what to expect from our future supplier.

Certifications and Standards

service provider who complies with industry certifications like ISO 27001 or the government’s Cyber Essentials Scheme. A structured process, good data governance and repair support are a number of the simplest industry practices to seem at a cloud service provider.

Reliability

By checking the performance of the cloud company for the past year, we will measure its reliability. search for the places where the corporate can handle both planned and unplanned downtime. this is often an important point to see because the data recovery during a disaster may be a huge responsibility
